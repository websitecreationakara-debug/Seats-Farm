About Us

Located in Kampot Province, Cambodia, S.E.A.T.S Farm is a beacon of sustainable agriculture and culinary brilliance. With a diverse range of organic vegetables, the farm empowers local farmers while cultivating the celebrated Kampot pepper.

At the core of S.E.A.T.S Farm's success is its dedication to empowering farmers with knowledge and modern techniques through workshops and hands-on training. The farm's wide selection of vegetables, including leafy greens and vibrant peppers, offers farm-to-table freshness.

The true star of S.E.A.T.S Farm is the revered Kampot pepper, a Protected Geographical Indication (PGI) product known for its unique and spicy taste. Cultivated organically with careful attention, this pepper has earned global recognition, making it a favorite of chefs and food enthusiasts.

Beyond its culinary contributions, S.E.A.T.S Farm supports the local community by generating employment opportunities and contributing to education and health initiatives — a harmonious blend of tradition and innovation in sustainable agriculture.
