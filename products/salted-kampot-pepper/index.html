Kampot Pepper — Salted Fresh Peppercorn

The True Taste of Salted Kampot Pepper

We carefully select slightly young peppercorns on our farm in Kampot to ensure a great taste all year round. The only ingredients are fresh peppercorns grown and harvested on our own farm, and natural sea salt from Kampot's own salt fields.

There are two types, “black” and “green,” depending on the processing method (with or without fermentation). Please enjoy its unique and delightful Cambodian flavor.

Naturally Crafted Kampot Salt

Our Salted Kampot Pepper is crafted with mineral-rich Kampot salt, sourced directly from local farmers. Every pinch reflects the authentic taste and tradition of this special region.
