About OEM

Inquiries About OEM Manufacturing and Procurement

S.E.A.T.S Farm is developing an agricultural business in Cambodia and has established a broad network, not only with our own farms but also across Cambodia, enabling us to collaborate with agricultural producers and food factories throughout the country. Our Japanese staff, highly knowledgeable in agricultural production and food distribution, support you in Japanese and handle procurement, production, and supply of raw materials and products that meet your needs. We also have an affiliated company in Japan that can handle yen-settled transactions.
