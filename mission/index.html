Our Mission

“From Cambodia, To The World With Us”

The above is the business mission of S.E.A.T.S Farm.

We create and produce valuable agricultural products together with the people of Cambodia, and send them out to the world. We aim to develop products that foreign consumers recognize as uniquely originating from Cambodia — not as a subcontractor of neighboring countries.

Each of us is proud to be involved in this business, and we work hard every day to realize it, aiming for the direction we should take in the world.

Delivering high-quality Kampot Pepper and agricultural products from Kampot Province, Cambodia, to Cambodia and the world.
