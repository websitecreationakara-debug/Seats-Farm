Introduction of S.E.A.T.S Farm

Delivering High-Quality Products from Cambodia to the World

S.E.A.T.S Farm is an agricultural production development project undertaken by S.E.A.T.S Inc., a Japanese trading company in Cambodia. We own a 6.5-hectare in-house farm in the Chuk area, Kampot province, southern Cambodia.

Here, we export and sell “Kampot Pepper” from S.E.A.T.S Farm, all cultivated on our own farm, to Japan and other countries. In order to create world-class products from Cambodia, we continue to take on challenges through trial and error every day, as well as produce and sell high-quality branded vegetables within Cambodia.

From Kampot Province, Cambodia

The Pepper the World's Chefs Recognize

Kampot pepper is considered a valuable product representing Kampot province, with a quality and spicy taste different from other peppers, recognized worldwide by famous French chefs. Kampot pepper is listed as a Protected Geographical Indication (PGI) product. Its unique taste comes from the fertile soil of Damrey Mountain combined with the cultivation techniques of the farmers who oversee every stage.

Our peppers are entirely organic — we use no chemical fertilizers of any kind, only cow and bat dung, and we harvest by hand, hand-picking only the highest-quality peppercorns.

S.E.A.T.S Farm supports the Sustainable Development Goals through its business activities.

How We Produce Kampot Pepper

A Taste Shaped by Nature and Tradition

Ideal Environment for Growing Pepper

The fertile soil in the Kampot region, surrounded by sea and mountains, is rich in minerals, and the tropical climate and heavy rainfall maximize the flavor of the pepper.

Traditional Farming Without Pesticides

S.E.A.T.S Farm is certified by Ecocert, a French international organic certification body, and faithfully follows traditional farming methods.

High Quality Through In-House Production

We handle everything from cultivation to delivery ourselves, applying our own strict standards on top of Kampot Pepper Association guidelines.
